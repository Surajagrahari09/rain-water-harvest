Here are some steps, how to rain water harvest?

STEP 1

Choose a catchment area. This is the area where the rainwater will be collected. It can be your roof, courtyard, or driveway. The catchment area should be clean and free of debris.

STEP 2

Install a gutter system. This will help to collect the rainwater from the catchment area and direct it to a storage tank.

STEP 3

Install a first flush diverter. This will divert the first bit of rainwater away from the storage tank. This is because the first bit of rainwater is typically the most polluted.

STEP 4

Install a filter system. This will help to remove any pollutants from the rainwater before it is stored.

STEP 5

Install an overflow valve. This will prevent the storage tank from overflowing during heavy rains.
